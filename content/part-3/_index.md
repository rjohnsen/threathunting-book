Investigative technique turns access to data into a defensible investigation. These notes focus on two foundations: understanding what the data can tell you, and reconstructing events without forcing them into a story too early.

{{% notice style="info" title="How to use this section" %}}
The techniques are investigation habits, not mechanical recipes. Preserve raw observations, make assumptions visible, and treat every pattern as something to test against context and alternative explanations.
